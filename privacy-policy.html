UX Issue Tracker - Privacy Policy

Last Updated: September 19, 2024
Simple Summary: UX Issue Tracker processes data locally in your browser and sends information directly to your JIRA instance. We do not collect, store, or access your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

We do NOT collect any personal information.

The extension processes the following data locally in your browser:

  • Screenshots you capture using the extension
  • Form data you enter (issue descriptions, ratings, etc.)
  • JIRA configuration settings you provide (URL, credentials)
  • Current webpage URL where you report issues

2. How We Use Information

All data processing occurs locally in your browser:

  • Screenshots: Processed locally, then sent directly to your JIRA instance
  • Form data: Formatted locally, then sent directly to your JIRA instance
  • JIRA credentials: Stored locally in your browser's secure storage
  • Page URLs: Included in JIRA issues for context

3. Data Storage

  • Local Storage: JIRA configuration settings are stored locally in your browser using Chrome's secure storage API
  • No Remote Storage: We do not store any of your data on our servers
  • No Tracking: We do not track your usage or browsing behavior

4. Data Sharing

We do not share your data with third parties.

Your data is only sent to:

  • Your JIRA Instance: Issue data and screenshots are sent directly to the JIRA instance you configure
  • No Analytics: We do not use analytics services or tracking tools
  • No Advertising: We do not share data with advertising networks

5. Third-Party Services

JIRA Integration

  • Extension communicates directly with your JIRA Cloud instance
  • All communication uses HTTPS encryption
  • We do not intercept or store this communication
  • JIRA's privacy policy applies to data stored in your JIRA instance

6. Data Security

  • HTTPS: All API communications use HTTPS encryption
  • Local Processing: Screenshots and sensitive data processed locally
  • Secure Storage: Browser's built-in secure storage for credentials
  • No Server Storage: No data stored on external servers

7. Your Rights

Since we don't collect or store your personal data:

  • Data Control: You have complete control over your data
  • Local Deletion: Uninstall the extension to remove all local data
  • JIRA Data: Contact your JIRA administrator for data in JIRA

8. Permissions Explained

The extension requests these Chrome permissions:

  • "activeTab": Access current webpage to capture screenshots
  • "storage": Store JIRA configuration locally
  • "scripting": Inject selection overlay on webpages
  • "<all_urls>": Work on any website you choose to report issues on

9. Children's Privacy

This extension is intended for business/professional use. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

10. Changes to Privacy Policy

We will update this privacy policy as needed. Changes will be posted with a new "Last Updated" date.
